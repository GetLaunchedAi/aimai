About Us

About AIMAI

AIMAI Auto Electronic Solutions is a trusted mobile installation business serving drivers, fleets, and equipment owners across Michigan. Since 2022, AIMAI has provided reliable electronic solutions for thousands of vehicles and heavy machinery—from personal cars and commercial fleets to generators and agricultural equipment.

Backed by professional certifications and years of field experience, AIMAI delivers intelligent, dependable, and innovative installations. Our mission is simple: engineer trust, restore confidence, and provide electronic solutions for anything powered by a battery and motor.
